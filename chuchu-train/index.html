ChuChu Train

Lay one rail. Ride every tile.

ChuChu Train is a one-stroke puzzle — draw a single connected rail that fills the whole board while visiting each numbered station in order. A cozy wooden-toy-train world on a playroom table, with rail gimmicks that stack up as levels climb.
